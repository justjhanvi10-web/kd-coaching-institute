Founder & Director • Chemistry Gold Medalist

Priyanka Batra Gakhar

For over 17 years, Priyanka Batra Gakhar has devoted herself to one calling — teaching. A Chemistry gold medalist and an M.Phil scholar from Delhi University, she brings the rare combination of deep academic distinction and a genuine love for guiding young minds.

Her philosophy is simple and unwavering: learning should never be compromised. It is this belief that shaped KD Coaching Institute into a place where students are taught not just to score, but to truly understand — to learn by heart.

What Drives Her

A passion for teaching, an inspiration from home

Beyond the medals and the research, what defines Priyanka is a simple, enduring passion for teaching. Every lesson is an opportunity to spark curiosity and build confidence in a young learner.

She credits her greatest inspiration to her parents — and especially her father — whose values continue to shape the way she teaches and leads KD Coaching Institute today.
